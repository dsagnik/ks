Who We Are

Kanishk Services is a multi-domain service firm dedicated to empowering careers and transforming businesses through comprehensive training, placement services, and strategic business solutions.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between talent and opportunity by providing world-class training, reliable placements, and strategic business support that drives growth and success.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and preferred partner for career development and business transformation across industries, setting new standards in quality and service excellence.

Our Values

Integrity, quality, innovation, accountability, and unwavering commitment to client satisfaction form the foundation of everything we do.

What We Do

IT & Non-IT Training

We offer comprehensive training programs designed to equip professionals with industry-relevant skills. Our practical, hands-on approach ensures participants are job-ready upon completion, with courses ranging from software development to business analytics.

Job Placement Services

Our extensive network connects skilled and unskilled professionals with leading employers. We facilitate placements across IT and non-IT sectors, ensuring the right fit for both candidates and organizations through our meticulous screening and matching process.

Business Advisory

We support business growth through strategic consulting, market guidance, and digital solutions development. Our experts help organizations navigate challenges, optimize operations, and establish a strong online presence through websites, blogs, and digital platforms.

End-to-End Mediation

We provide comprehensive coordination services ensuring smooth communication and collaboration between clients and service providers. Our mediation expertise guarantees seamless project execution and client satisfaction.
